About Us

Our 3D Printing Service

At Print2Go, we specialize in professional-grade 3D printing for hobbyists, engineers, designers, and businesses. Whether you need a single prototype or a small production run, we offer a variety of materials (PLA, ABS, PETG, resin, and more) and finishes to suit your project.
Upload your STL or OBJ files, choose your print settings, and we’ll take care of the rest – from slicing to shipping. Not sure how to prepare your file? We’ve got easy guides and expert support available.

Custom 3D Design – Built From Your Vision

If you don’t have a 3D model yet, we can create one for you. Whether you have a sketch, an idea in your head, or just a rough reference, our team will transform it into a detailed, print-ready 3D design.
We can also replicate existing parts, help bring product concepts to life, or modify and repair broken models.
You'll be able to review and approve the model before it's printed, ensuring it fits your expectations and project goals.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What file types do you accept for 3D printing?

We accept STL, OBJ, and 3MF files. If you’re not sure about your file format, just send us what you have—we’ll help convert or fix it.

What materials can I choose from?

We offer PLA, ABS, PETG, TPU (flexible), and resin. Each has different properties like strength, flexibility, or surface finish. Contact us if you’re not sure what to choose.

Can you help me create a 3D model from an idea or sketch?

Yes! Our modeling team can design a 3D file from scratch based on your input. Send us a drawing, photo, or even a description, and we’ll get started.

Can I make changes to my model after submitting it?

Yes, you can request changes after submission—just contact us as soon as possible. If we haven’t started printing yet, we can adjust the file or settings. For custom modeling projects, we include revision rounds so you can review and fine-tune your design before it's finalized.

How long does it take to complete an order?

Most orders are completed within 2–5 business days, depending on the complexity, material, and size. Custom 3D modeling projects may take additional time based on the level of detail and revisions required. Rush options are available—just let us know your deadline when placing the order.

Where is this service available?

Print2Go currently operates in Portugal and Spain. We offer reliable local shipping with tracking and delivery estimates. More regions may be added soon as we grow!
