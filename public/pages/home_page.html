Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is 3D Printing?

3D printing, also known as additive manufacturing, is a process where material is deposited layer by layer to build a 3D object based on a digital model.

How long does it take to print an object?

The time required to 3D print an object depends on several factors including the size, complexity, and resolution of the object. Smaller objects can be printed in a few hours, while larger and more detailed ones may take several days.

What file formats are accepted for 3D printing?

We accept commonly used 3D model file formats such as STL, OBJ, and 3MF. Please make sure your file is properly formatted to avoid any printing issues.

Do you offer custom 3D modeling services?

Yes! We offer custom 3D modeling services to help bring your ideas to life. Our team of experienced designers can create 3D models based on your specifications.

What materials can be used for 3D printing?

We offer a variety of materials for 3D printing including PLA, ABS, PETG, and more. Each material has its own properties such as strength, flexibility, and appearance, so we can help choose the best material for your project.
